Sourdough

Description

Sourdough bread is made by fermenting the yeast naturally present in flour. It's said to be healthier and more nutritious than regular bread

Ingredients

  • 3/4 Cup Warm Water
  • 1 Cup Sourdough Starter
  • 1.5 Teaspoons Active dry yeast
  • 3 Cups Bread Floor

Steps

  1. Mix all the Ingredients in a bowl
  2. Transfer it into a baking dish and rest for 2 hours at room temperature
  3. Bake in oven at 230 Celsius for 45 minutes
  4. Your Sourdough is ready!
